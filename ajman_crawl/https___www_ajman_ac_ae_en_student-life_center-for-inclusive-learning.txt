Vision of the Center for Inclusive Learning
The Center for Inclusive Learning aims to serve as a leading example of inclusivity in higher education. Our vision is to establish a universally accessible campus that promotes the full engagement and contribution of every student, creating an academic experience and environment that is enriching for all.
Our Mission
The mission of the Center for Inclusive Learning is to ensure equitable access to educational opportunities by promoting an inclusive community that supports students with disabilities. Through this center, we aim to build a supportive, empowering environment that enables every student to achieve success.

Services We Offer
The Center for Inclusive Learning provides accessible, equitable, and inclusive academic experiences for students with self-disclosed disabilities through the following services:
Learning Access Plan Services:
Tailored accommodations to provide students with full access to the academic environment. These include classroom adjustments, exam modifications, and adaptive equipment to support diverse needs.
Exam Modifications:
Proctored exams for students requiring extended time or specialized equipment, ensuring a supportive and fair testing environment.
Alternative Text Formats:
Audio and electronic textbook formats to assist students with print or visual disabilities.
Assistive Technology:
Advanced tools and software for students with various disabilities, including screen readers, Braille printers, and specialized keyboards.
