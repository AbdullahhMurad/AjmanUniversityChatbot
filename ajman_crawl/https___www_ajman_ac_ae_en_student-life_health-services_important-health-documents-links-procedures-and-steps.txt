Submitting the Medical Fitness Certificate on ORS
Kindly collect your completed medical fitness certificate
(medical checkup main page with the student photo)
and
Submit it on ORS - E Request by following the cycle below:
Open ORS system (ors.ajman.ac.ae)
Enter your username and password (Your username is your AU ID)
Click on E-Requests
Go to “All Requests” on the left pane menu
Click on New Request
Go to “
Request Category
”
Select
Admission
Go to “
Request type”
Select “
Submission of the Medical Checkup Report Document”
Then proceed.
Once the request has been submitted, the office of Medical Services will validate the document, then will send it to office of Admission & Registration, and student
will receive a notification of completion.
