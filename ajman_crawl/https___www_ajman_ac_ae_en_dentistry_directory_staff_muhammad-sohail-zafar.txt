CV
Prof. Muhammad Zafar earned his Ph.D. from Nottingham Trent University (2011) and his MSc from Queen Mary University of London (2007), UK. With extensive teaching experience and a distinguished research record in dentistry, he has worked in several countries, including the UK, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Jordan, and Pakistan.

Prof. Zafar is a member and fellow of esteemed professional organizations, including the Higher Education Academy (FHEA), the Faculty of Dental Trainers (FDTFEd), and the Royal College of Physicians and Surgeons of Glasgow (MFDSRCPSG). He has authored numerous articles, books, and book chapters, with research interests spanning clinical dentistry, oral health, regenerative medicine, and advanced biomaterials for dental applications.
