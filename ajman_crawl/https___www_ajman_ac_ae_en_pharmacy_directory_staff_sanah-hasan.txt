CV
Dr. Sanah Hasan earned her PhD in 2013 from Monash University, Australia. She also holds a PharmD (2002) and a BS in Pharmacy (1994) from the School of Pharmacy at the University of Kansas, USA.

With extensive experience in hospital pharmacy and community pharmacy research, Dr. Hasan has held faculty positions at several academic institutions before joining Ajman University. She has been an invited speaker at numerous regional and international conferences and currently serves as the principal investigator for several research projects focusing on pharmacy practice and community pharmacy services in the UAE and the broader region.
