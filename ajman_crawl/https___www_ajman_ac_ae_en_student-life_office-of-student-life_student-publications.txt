Student Publications
Student publications allow students to share their experiences and success stories during their university life in order to create a stimulating and inspiring environment. The Office of Student Life oversees the publication of the students e-magazine in both Arabic and English, at the end of each semester. It is worth mentioning that the articles take into account the diversity of topics and experiences. The magazine is sent to all students via email.
