Office of Student Life
As a student at Ajman University, you become part of a diverse and culturally rich community. The Office of Student Life plays a vital role in creating an exceptional experience by organizing various purposeful activities, events and programs that enhance students’ personality, refine their skills and develop their talents. It strives to foster an environment of inclusiveness that promotes academic excellence. Don’t miss the chance to join our dynamic and extraordinary community!
The Office of Student Life offers a wide range of programs, events and activities that cover all aspects of life and cater for the needs and interests of students, including:
