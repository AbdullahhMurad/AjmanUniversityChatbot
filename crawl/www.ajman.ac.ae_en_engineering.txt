Dean's Message
Welcome to the College of Engineering and Information Technology (CEIT) at Ajman University, where innovation meets education to shape the future. Established in 1988, CEIT has grown to become one of the most dynamic colleges in our institution. As you explore the possibilities within our college, I extend a warm welcome to prospective students, parents, and visitors alike.
Our mission at CEIT is to provide a cutting-edge educational experience in the fields of engineering and information technology. We are committed to nurturing the next generation of engineers and IT professionals, equipped with the skills and knowledge needed for thriving careers. In our pursuit of excellence, we have crafted an educational environment that fosters active learning, ensuring that our students are well-prepared to face the challenges of a rapidly evolving world.
To achieve this, we embrace student-centered teaching and learning methodologies, emphasizing academic rigor and hands-on practice. Our state-of-the-art facilities, constantly updated to keep pace with industry trends, provide our students with the required hands-on and practical experience. Furthermore, we provide our students with internship opportunities that empower them to seamlessly transition into professional practice upon graduation.
At CEIT, we pride ourselves on offering a vibrant and unique educational experience. Our programs, both at the undergraduate and graduate levels, are designed to meet the demands of a dynamic job market. All our programs are accredited by the Ministry of Education in the UAE. The Electrical and Biomedical programs are accredited by the ABET accreditation body.
Our undergraduate programs span a wide range of disciplines, including Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Biomedical Engineering, Information Technology (with a focus on networking and security), Information Systems (specializing in E-Business Management and Project Management), Data Analytics, Civil Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering. For those who aspire to earn a higher degree, our graduate program in Artificial Intelligence will be an excellent choice.
Diversity is at the heart of our community, and we cherish it as a fundamental element in our journey toward inclusiveness and excellence. Our student body and faculty hail from diverse backgrounds, enriching the academic discourse and promoting a global perspective.
I am deeply proud to be a part of our remarkable team of faculty members and staff, all of whom are dedicated to the success of our students. Together, we spare no effort in providing unwavering support, guidance, and mentorship to help students achieve their educational objectives.
Whether you are a prospective student exploring your academic options, a parent seeking the best education for your child, or a visitor curious about our campus, I invite you to delve into the pages of this website or, better yet, experience our beautiful campus in Ajman firsthand. Discover the limitless possibilities that await you at CEIT, where we are shaping tomorrow's innovators, creators, and leaders. Welcome to the future of engineering and information technology education at Ajman University!
Dr. Mohamed Nasor, Dean.
