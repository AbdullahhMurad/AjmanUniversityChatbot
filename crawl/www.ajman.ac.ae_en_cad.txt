Dean's Message
The college of Architecture Art and Design (CAAD) is one of the most comprehensive colleges of its kind in the region. Our programs span through all aspects of Architecture, Interior Design, Building Engineering and Construction Management, and Urban Design. We pride ourselves in programs that are responsive to market demands. Our programs go through rigorous continuous improvement processes and they are all accredited by the Commission for Academic Accreditation (CAA) of the Ministry of Education. Our Bachelor of Architecture program is validated by the International Union of Architects (UNESCO-UIA) and our Interior Design program is a member of the International Federation of Interior Designers and the Association of Professional Interior Designers.
We take the placements of our graduates very seriously. Through our alumni network and the industrial advisory board, we do our best to place our graduates in their area of interest.
Our outstanding faculty members and state of the art facility foster an environment that is conducive to innovation. Through our students’ exchange programs and partnerships with international universities, the learning experience is extraordinary. Once you step into our space you will experience the passion, the energy, and motivation of our students, faculty and, staff. This unparalleled passion is contagious! That is why so many students made our college their choice.
Join us and become part of our family.
Riad Saraiji, Ph.D.
Dean
